# Privacy Policy — FocusForge

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **FocusForge** ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how FocusForge collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using FocusForge, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

1. **Provide and maintain** the FocusForge service
2. **Sync your data** across devices via Firebase Cloud Firestore
3. **Calculate productivity statistics** and streak tracking
4. **Send session completion notifications** (with your permission)
5. **Diagnose crashes** and improve app stability via Firebase Crashlytics
6. **Analyze aggregate usage** to improve features and user experience
7. **Comply with legal obligations** and enforce our Terms of Service

We **do NOT** use your data for:
- Third-party advertising targeting
- Sale of personal data to third parties
- Automated decision-making with legal effects

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

All user data is stored securely on **Google Firebase** infrastructure (Cloud Firestore), which is hosted on Google Cloud Platform servers with international data centers.

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security practices:
- **Encryption in transit:** TLS 1.3 for all data transmissions
- **Encryption at rest:** Firebase's default AES-256 encryption
- **Authentication:** JWT-based Firebase Auth tokens
- **Firestore Rules:** Server-side security rules ensure users can only access their own data
- **Access Control:** Limited internal access to production databases

### 4.3 Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account data | Until account deletion |
| Session history | 2 years from session date |
| Analytics data (aggregated) | 14 months |
| Crash logs | 90 days |

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### 5.1 General Rights (All Users)
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Portability:** Request export of your data in a machine-readable format

### 5.2 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent at any time
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

### 5.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

### 5.4 How to Exercise Your Rights
Send a request to: **support@focusforge.app** with subject "Privacy Request"
We will respond within **30 days**.

You can also delete your account directly in the app: **Profile → Settings → Delete Account**
