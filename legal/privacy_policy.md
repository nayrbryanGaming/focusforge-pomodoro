# Privacy Policy for FocusForge

**Last Updated: April 21, 2026**

FocusForge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by FocusForge.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
- **Email Address**: Collected when you create an account for synchronization.
- **Usage Data**: We collect information on how you use the app, such as session duration, task completion, and feature engagement.

### 1.2 Device Information
- We collect basic device info (model, OS version) for crash reporting and performance optimization via Firebase Crashlytics.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the FocusForge service.
- To synchronize your tasks and statistics across devices.
- To improve the application through analytics (Firebase Analytics).
- To detect, prevent, and address technical issues (Firebase Crashlytics).

## 3. Data Storage and Security
We use Google Firebase for data storage. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Firebase**: For authentication, database, analytics, and crash reporting.
- **Google Play Services**: For application distribution and licensing.

## 5. User Rights
- **Access and Portability**: You can request a copy of your data.
- **Deletion**: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy
FocusForge does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information.
