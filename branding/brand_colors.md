# FocusForge Brand Identity

## Design Philosophy
FocusForge represents the intersection of ancient craftsmanship and modern cognitive science. The brand should feel **precise, powerful, and premium**.

## Color Palette

### Primary: Deep Forge Orange
- **Hex**: `#FF6B35`
- **Use**: Main calls to action, active focus state, logo accent.

### Secondary: Dark Space Navy
- **Hex**: `#0F172A`
- **Use**: Backgrounds, high-contrast surfaces, premium typography.

### Accent: Solar Glow
- **Hex**: `#FFD166`
- **Use**: Achievement badges, special highlights, break-state transitions.

## Typography
- **Primary**: *Outfit* (Geometric, modern, high-readability).
- **Secondary**: *JetBrains Mono* (Used sparingly for stats and "Forging" indicators to give a technical edge).

## Visual Style
- **Glassmorphism**: 10-15% opacity white surfaces with high blur (sigma 10-20).
- **Gradients**: Linear gradients from `#FF6B35` to `#FFD166` at 45 degrees.
- **Animations**: Ease-out cubic curves, subtle 20-30px slide-ups on entry.
